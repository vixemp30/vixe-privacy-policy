# Privacy Policy for Vixe Music Player **Effective Date:** April 23, 2026 **Last Updated:** April 23, 2026 ## 1. Introduction Welcome to Vixe Music Player ("we", "our", or "the App"). Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our Android application. By using Vixe Music Player, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy. ## 2. Information We Collect Vixe Music Player is designed with privacy as a priority. We **do not collect, store, or share** any personal information from your device, such as your name, email address, phone number, or location. The App only accesses the following types of data locally on your device: ### a) Music & Audio Files - **What we access:** Audio files (MP3, M4A, WAV, OGG, FLAC, etc.) stored on your device's internal storage or SD card. - **Why we need it:** To scan, list, play, edit (merge, split, remove silence), and manage your music library within the App. - **How it is handled:** All processing happens **entirely on your device**. We never upload, transmit, or share your music files to any server or third party. ### b) Storage (Photos/Media) - **What we access:** When you choose to change the app background or album cover, we may access images from your gallery. - **Why we need it:** To set custom background images or embed album art into your music files. - **How it is handled:** Images are processed locally. Album art embedding uses FFmpeg locally on your device. No images are sent to external servers (unless you choose to download a cover from a URL you provide). ### c) Device Identifiers (for Ads) - **What we collect:** Google AdMob may collect your Android Advertising ID. - **Why we need it:** To serve relevant advertisements and prevent ad fraud. - **How it is handled:** This is collected by Google's advertising SDK. You can reset or limit this ID at any time via your device's Google Settings app. ## 3. How We Use Your Information We use the information accessed by the App **only** for the following core features: - Playing and controlling audio playback. - Creating playlists and navigating your music folders. - Equalizer and audio effects (Bass Boost, Virtualizer, Loudness Enhancer). - Editing audio (merge, split, remove silence). - Customizing the app interface (background images, accent colors, album covers). - Displaying advertisements (via Google AdMob). ## 4. Third-Party Services Vixe Music Player uses the following third-party services, which may collect information under their own privacy policies: | Service | Purpose | Privacy Policy | | :--- | :--- | :--- | | **Google AdMob** | Displaying advertisements | [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy) | | **Google Play Billing** | In-app purchases (Premium features) | [Google Payments TOS](https://payments.google.com/payments/terms) | | **FFmpegKit** | Audio editing (merge/split/silence removal) | Processed 100% offline – no data sent | | **Glide** | Image loading & caching | Processed locally | | **UCrop** | Image cropping for album art | Processed locally | We do **not** share your music files, folders, or personal data with any third party. ## 5. Permissions Explained The App requests the following permissions: | Permission | When Used | Why Required | | :--- | :--- | :--- | | `READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE` / `READ_MEDIA_AUDIO` | First launch | To scan and play music files from your device. | | `WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE` (Android 9 and below) | Audio editing | To save merged/split/processed audio files. | | `MANAGE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE` (Android 11+) | Deleting files | To permanently delete song files from your device (optional feature). | | `POST_NOTIFICATIONS` (Android 13+) | During playback | To display the music player notification with playback controls. | | `FOREGROUND_SERVICE` | During playback | To keep music playing when the app is in the background. | | `INTERNET` | Ads & online covers | To load ads (AdMob) or download an album cover from a URL you provide. | You can deny any permission, but some features may not work properly. ## 6. Data Storage and Security - **All your music, playlists, and settings are stored locally** on your device in the app's private storage or your device's external storage. - We do not operate any servers to store user data. - Audio processing (merge, split, silence removal) is done entirely offline using FFmpegKit – no internet access is required for these features. ## 7. Children's Privacy Vixe Music Player does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The App simply plays music files that parents or guardians may have stored on the device. If you believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us. ## 8. Your Choices and Control - **Limit Ads:** You can reset your Android Advertising ID or opt out of personalized ads in your device's Google Settings. - **Delete App Data:** Uninstalling the App removes all app-specific settings and cache. Your original music files remain untouched. - **Manage Permissions:** You can revoke permissions at any time via your device's Settings > Apps > Vixe Music Player > Permissions. ## 9. Changes to This Privacy Policy We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last Updated" date at the top of this page indicates when changes were made. We encourage you to review this page periodically. ## 10.
